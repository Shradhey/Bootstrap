About Us


Every four years, we look at the dismal performance of India at Olympics. 3 generations have passed hoping India to qualify for Football World Cup at least once. Promising people in the industry seldom get the infrastructure and platform they deserve. Boasting of about a sixth of world’s population, this certainly doesn’t look good upon us.


Everyone in team Khelacademy believes this way of living has to absolutely change. By connecting students with those worthy to train them, we are hoping to fill a big void. We also leverage internet, data mining, statistical analysis to monitor each one of our participant, and help them with substantial improvements

What We Do

  • Provide a platform for nurturing prospective champions.
  • Efficiently manage our sessions without disturbing the typical routine of a student.
  • Statistical Analysis at regular intervals of every student enrolled.
  • Regular tournaments of their respective sports to infuse a competitive spirit.
  • Contribute to a healthy development of a child by following a well researched workout which is proved to be extremely efficient.
